Sword

Swords damage scales primarily with strength but also benefits from dexterity. Swords can be used in combination with a shield. You will be able to use two masteries: The Swordmaster (attacks that hinder target) and The Defender (defensive abilities).

Strengths

  • Swords excel in close-quarters combat.
  • Swords are the only weapon that can be wielded with a shield.
  • While equipped with a shield, a sword user can block enemy attacks more effectively.

Weaknesses

  • Swords lack range and reach.
  • Sword abilities have long cooldowns
×

Musket

Muskets' damage scale primarily with Dexterity but also scales with Intelligence. You can take two masteries the Sharpshooter and the Trapper.

Strengths

  • High damage
  • Headshots deal extra damage

Weaknesses

  • Long reload time
  • Requires careful aiming
  • Requires ammo for every shot
