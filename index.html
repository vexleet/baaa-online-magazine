About New World

New World is a new MMORPG game that came out recently to the web stores by Amazon Games. Currently It’s one of the most played games on Steam and it’s the biggest MMO release for 2021.

This Website has a leveling guide and also information about the different weapons and professions in the game. Scroll down to see all the information you would need to make you play the game and make sure to check out our leveling guide Leveling Guide

Be Anyone in Aeternum

When you hit the shores of Aeternum and you get to your first settlement you can become anyone in the world of New World. From a shipwrecker you can become a lumberjack, a blacksmith, a fighter, the possibilties are endless and you can always change your playstyle as you are not stopped of playing something different. Come to the beatuiful supernatual world of Aeternum and forge the destiny you want!
